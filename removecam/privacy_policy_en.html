2. Information We Collect

Important: RemoveCam does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

2.1 Photos and Images

  • RemoveCam requires access to photos you select or capture for processing purposes only
  • All image processing (background removal, blur, object removal, and text recognition) is performed entirely on your device
  • Processed images are stored locally on your device only
  • We do NOT upload any images to external servers

2.2 Local Storage

  • The app stores processed images in your device's local storage for your convenience
  • You have full control to delete these images at any time through the app or your device's file manager
  • Image history is maintained locally and is deleted when you uninstall the app

3. Permissions We Request

3.1 Camera Permission

  • Purpose: To capture photos for image processing
  • Usage: Only activated when you choose to take a photo within the app

3.2 Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To read photos from your gallery and save processed images
  • Usage: Required for selecting images and saving your edited results

3.3 Internet Permission

  • Purpose: To display this Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • Usage: Only used when you choose to view legal documents within the app
  • Note: No user data or images are transmitted over the internet

4. How We Use Your Information

Your photos and images are used solely for the following purposes:

  • Background removal processing
  • Background blur effects
  • Object removal (magic remove)
  • Text recognition (OCR)
  • Saving processed results to your device

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain any external servers or databases
  • Your images are protected by your device's security measures
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
